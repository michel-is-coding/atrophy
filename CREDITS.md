# Credits

The concept of **"prompt fordism"** (the fordism of prompting) comes from the essay
*Prompting is the New Fordism* by [Jad1908](https://github.com/Jad1908) (Medium: `@cafc.aouad.jad`):
https://medium.com/@cafc.aouad.jad/prompting-is-the-new-fordism-c393df41d3cf

That essay describes how prompting can turn knowledge work into assembly-line execution.
`atrophy` is an attempt to measure what the essay describes: whether you are still
exercising judgment, or running on autopilot in front of the machine.
